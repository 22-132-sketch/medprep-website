What's Coming

The Leaderboard
Worth Competing For.

Here is exactly what the MedPREP Leaderboard will track, rank, and celebrate.

Weekly & All-Time Rankings

Rankings reset weekly so every student has a fresh chance to climb. All-time rankings track your consistency across the entire session.

Public Student Profiles

Every ranked student gets a public profile — year of study, subject strengths, MCQs attempted, accuracy rate, and earned badges.

Subject-wise Rankings

Not just overall — rank by individual subject. Be the top Pharmacology student in KMU, or dominate Pathology in your year.

Achievement Badges

Earn badges for streaks, speed, accuracy milestones, and subject mastery. Displayed publicly on your profile for the whole university to see.

Streak Tracking

Consistent daily study is tracked and rewarded. Students with the longest active streaks get featured prominently on the leaderboard.

University Expansion

Starting with KMU. As MedPREP expands to UHS and beyond, the leaderboard will grow into a national ranking of Pakistan's top medical students.
